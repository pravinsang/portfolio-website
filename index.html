About Me

As the Head of the Electronics and Computer Engineering Department at the Institute of Engineering, Purwanchal Campus, I am deeply committed to teaching and learning in the field of programming and computer science. With a Master’s degree in Information System Engineering, I have had the opportunity to teach various programming and technical subjects, including C Programming, Python, Data Structures and Algorithms, Design and Analysis of Algorithms, Compiler Design and Construction, Database Management Systems, and Program Analysis. My passion for programming and technology fuels my drive to stay updated with the latest advancements in the field. I enjoy sharing my knowledge with students and helping them build a strong technical foundation that blends theory with practical applications. In my teaching career, I have cultivated a learning environment where students are encouraged to explore, innovate, and think critically. Teaching is more than a profession for me—it's a passion that I continuously grow through learning from both my students and the ever-changing world of technology..
